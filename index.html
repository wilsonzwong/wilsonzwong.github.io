Wilson Wong is a journalist and writer based in Brooklyn.

He is a Kundiman Creative Nonfiction Fellow and staff reporter at NBC News digital, where he covers breaking news, sexual assault, race, higher education, animals, and more.

He is working on projects about intergenerational trauma, queer Asian American history and gay bachelor enclaves. He also appeared on MSNBC once to talk about Lady Gaga’s stolen French bulldogs.
